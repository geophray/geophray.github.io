White Space & Clean Design

Asana

asana.com

Asana's home page is a great example of using white space and a clean design. As you scroll down the image above, you'll see how each element has enough empty space around it to make the site feel clean and uncluttered. There is plenty of breathing room.

Hick's Law

Neil Patel

neilpatel.com

Neil's website exemplifies Hick's Law by displaying only three very simple sections with clear instructions about how to get help with marketing. By offering only a few choices of what to do, it helps visitors avoid getting paralyzed and to make a decision.

Repetition & Proximity

Yoast SEO

yoast.com

Yoast's website did a fantastic job of grouping different related elements on their site and using great repetition within those groupings. You'll see that their CTA's near the top all look very similar, and further down the page all the links to plugins have repetition used in their images.
