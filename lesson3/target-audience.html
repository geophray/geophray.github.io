Target Audience

Persona 1

Photo by Zoe Deal on Unsplash

Kallie Wilkins

Kallie is a 36 year old woman with a green thumb who lives in Soda Springs, Idaho with her husband and six young children. She dreams of owning a garden center and nursery but for now she spends her spare time in the incredible garden in her back yard. She spends every waking hour with her hands in the dirt encouraging plants to grow. She'll rely heavily on accurate weather data to know when and how much to fertilize and water all her many plants.

Scenarios

  • Will the temperature drop below freezing tonight?
  • Should I turn off the sprinklers for a few days?
  • If I plant now will my seedlings and starts survive or is it too early?
  • Is this weekend going to be cool enough to apply another round of fertilizer?
  • Is it warm enough for the kids to run through the sprinklers today?

Persona 2

Photo by Jeremy Thomas on Unsplash

Jake Oliver

Jake is a 17 year old young man who lives in Preston, Idaho with his grandma, his two older brothers, and their pet llama. He is the youth leader at his local church group and as such he plans a lot of outdoor activities. He'll rely on the up-to-date local weather information on a weekly basis as he and the other youth leaders plan fun and engaging activities in the Southeast Idaho wilderness.
